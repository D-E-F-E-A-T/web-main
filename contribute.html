Contribute, Learn, Enjoy, and Smile

Join the Cause

Orchidware.net ("O.N" for short) is dedicated to maintaining the fun and frivolity of the art of programming. Contributors to this project enjoy learning, programming, sharing their knowledge and experience with others, and because they want to give back.

O.N is always seeking contributors. O.N contributors do this for fun, not for pay because these are intended to be side-projects in which they can gain new skills, learn from others, and maintain their inner "geek-dom" for at least a few hours per week outside their regular jobs or school work.

So, if you'd like to contribute, please send me a little note. O.N is seeking programmers, testers, artists, imaginative story-writers.

This site was implemented with HTML5, CSS, Javascript, JQueryUI.
