Philosophy

Join, Learn, Enjoy, and Smile

The Orchidware Project is dedicated to maintaining the fun and innocence of the art of programming.

Contributors enjoy learning, programming, sharing their knowledge and experience with others, and because they want to give back.

Members do this for fun, not for pay because these are intended to be side-projects in which they can gain new skills, learn from others, and maintain their inner "geek-dom" for at least a few hours per week outside their regular jobs or school work.

Individuals that would like to explore the software development field are also welcomed.
