Classroom Challenge

Educational games for the high-school and college-level classroom.

Using Classroom Challenge, high school and college-level students and teachers can create fun, interactive, engaging, and educational presentations for their classrooms.

Students and teachers create question and answer content specifically for their presentations. The content can then be played by students through several fun end exciting game formats.
